Andre

QUEENS

THE MUSICIAN

Andre is twenty seven and living in Queens where his days are split between working at a record store and making music. His night are alive, being a DJ in the making, crafting beats in his studio bedroom and throwing undergroung parties.

Restless and ambitious, Andre dreams of breaking into the city's music scene, even as family pressures try to persuade him in other directions. For him, every track is a chanc to turn noise into meaning, making his mark on New York City.

He spends his commute times on the train listening and making music, travelling all through New York to perform.
