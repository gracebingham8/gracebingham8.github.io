ROSA

MANNHATTAN

THE ARCHIVIST

Rosa is an archivist on the Upper West Side of Manhattan, spending days surrounded by dusty books and forgotten letters, fragments of other people's lives. At 62, she carries the weight of memory, both the city's and her own.

Recently divorced and quietly navigating solitude, Rosa finds solace in preserving the past, even as she wrestles with what to do with her own unwritten future. She searches for meaning and connection in a city that never stops changing.
